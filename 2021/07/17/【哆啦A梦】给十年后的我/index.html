【哆啦A梦】给十年后的我

Created2021-07-17|Updated2022-12-15|Videos
|Post View:

“That young man would be happy for the happiness of others and sad for their misfortune, which is the most important thing for a person”
Nobita seems to be a very weak child, but he is actually the best child in the world, and his potential is not shown in academics and sports, but in his courage in times of crisis, and his kind of pure love for people and things.
The key is to have a pure and kind heart, to grieve for the misfortunes of others and to rejoice in their happiness!

“那个年轻人会为别人幸福而高兴，为别人的不幸而伤心，这对于一个人来说是最重要的东西”
大雄看似是个很弱的孩子，但他其实是世界上最优秀的孩子，他的潜质不表现在学业与体育，而表现在危急时刻的勇气，以及他对人和物的一种纯真的爱。
最关键的是能有一颗纯洁善良的心，为别人的不幸而悲伤，为别人幸福而高兴！
